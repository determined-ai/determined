###############################
 Hyperparameter Search: Random
###############################

The ``random`` search method generates ``max_trials`` trials with
hyperparameters chosen uniformly at random from the configured
hyperparameter space. Each trial is trained for the number of units
specified by ``max_length`` (see :ref:`Training Units
<experiment-configuration_training_units>`) and then then the trial's
validation metrics are computed.
