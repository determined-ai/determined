**NEW FEATURES**

-  Support [`mmdetection`](https://github.com/open-mmlab/mmdetection), a
   popular library for object detection, in Determined. `mmdetection`
   allows users to easily train state-of-the-art object detection
   models; with Determined, users can take things one step further with
   cutting edge distributed training and hyperparameter search to
   further boost performance.
